Everything in one platform.

Every module is connected — data entered in TNI flows through to calendar, attendance, coverage, and reports automatically.

TNI Analysis
Upload your Training Need Identification sheet. Fuzzy-matches programme names against the master list, flags mismatches, and lets you confirm before saving.
TNI UPLOAD
Training Calendar
Plan sessions with auto-generated codes (Unit/Type/Number/FY/Batch). Tracks planned vs conducted status. Locks editing once a session is marked Conducted.
CALENDAR PLANNING
QR Attendance & Feedback
Generate QR codes for any session. Employees scan on any mobile — no app needed. Attendance auto-creates a record. Feedback QR captures a structured survey in Hindi or English. Optional 4-digit PIN locks attendance to authorised participants only.
LIVE QR SCANNING
Training Records
Individual attendance records per employee per session. Bulk upload via Excel. Session code auto-fills programme name, type, mode from Calendar.
ATTENDANCE RECORDS
Monthly Summary
Auto-calculates No. of Programmes, Person Seats, Man-Hours, and Coverage % by collar and type. Filters by month. Includes central-attended sessions.
ANALYTICS
Programme Details
Record conducted session actuals — dates, faculty, cost, feedback scores. Saving a record auto-updates Calendar status to Conducted.
SESSION ACTUALS
Feedback Reports
Per-session reaction survey analysis — gradient score bars by content, faculty, and venue; programme & trainer sub-totals; CSAT index; and open-ended learnings and suggestions.
FEEDBACK ANALYSIS
Central Calendar
Cross-plant sessions planned by the corporate team. Attendance pulls employees from any plant via QR scan. Auto-updates Programme Master for all attending plants.
CORPORATE TRAINING
Programme Master
Canonical programme list per plant. The gatekeeper — programmes must exist here before use in Calendar, Attendance, or Session records. Sync from TNI or add manually.
MASTER DATA
Excel Export
One-click export of attendance records, session details, Monthly Summary, and TNI data into formatted Excel workbooks with styled headers and auto column widths.
DATA EXPORT
Employee Master
Per-plant headcount with collar classification (Blue / White Collared), grade, department, section. Bulk import from Excel. Drives coverage % calculations.
HEADCOUNT MANAGEMENT
Programme Intelligence
Cross-programme analytics — training hours by type, coverage trends, top programmes by attendance. Central dashboard gives all-plant overview for admin.
